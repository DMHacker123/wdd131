Site Plan for "The Art of Minimalist Living"

This document outlines the website structure, including content purpose and selected color scheme.

Purpose of the Website

"The Art of Minimalist Living" serves as a resource on minimalism, offering guides, tips, and resources to simplify life and focus on essentials.

Page Descriptions

  • Home: Overview of minimalism, mission statement, and benefits.
  • Decluttering Tips: Practical steps for organizing physical and digital spaces.
  • Mindfulness & Well-being: How minimalism impacts mental health.
  • Contact Us: Contact form and newsletter sign-up for user interaction.

Color Scheme and Font Choices

The selected colors are chosen to enhance readability and visual simplicity:

  • Light Gray (#f4f4f4): Background for entire site for contrast and clarity.

    Used as the main background color across pages for a soft, neutral look.

  • Dark Green (#2E8B57): Header, footer, and button color for a cohesive brand feel.

    Adds contrast in header, footer, and buttons, creating a calming and natural aesthetic.

  • White (#ffffff): Main content background for legibility.

    Background for main sections, such as main and images, to keep focus on content.

  • Dark Charcoal (#333333): Main text color for contrast on light backgrounds.

    Primary font color for body text and headings, ensuring high readability.

  • Medium Green (#3CB371): Hover color for interactive elements.

    Enhances user interaction by changing color on hover for links and buttons.

  • Light Gray Border (#ccc): Border for input fields and images.

    Used to subtly outline elements, such as input fields, for a polished look.

Font: Roboto, imported from Google Fonts, provides a modern and clean typography style that aligns with the minimalist theme.

Typography and Layout

  • Header: Uses dark green (#2E8B57) with white text for contrast and readability.
  • Main Content: Dark charcoal text (#333333) on a white background (#ffffff) for clarity.
  • Navigation Links: White text on dark green (#2E8B57) with a hover effect in medium green (#3CB371).
  • Footer: Dark green background (#2E8B57) with white text to maintain consistency with the header.

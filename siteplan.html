Site Plan for "The Art of Minimalist Living"

This document outlines the website structure, including content purpose, selected color scheme, and font usage.

Purpose of the Website

"The Art of Minimalist Living" serves as a resource on minimalism, offering guides, tips, and resources to simplify life and focus on essentials.

Page Descriptions

  • Home: Overview of minimalism, mission statement, and benefits.
  • Decluttering Tips: Practical steps for organizing physical and digital spaces.
  • Mindfulness & Well-being: How minimalism impacts mental health.
  • Contact Us: Contact form and newsletter sign-up for user interaction.

Color Scheme and Font Choices

The selected colors and fonts are chosen to enhance readability and visual simplicity.

Colors:

  • Light Gray (#f4f4f4): Background color for the body for contrast and clarity.

    Main background color across pages, providing a soft, neutral tone.

  • Dark Green (#2E8B57): Used in the header, footer, and button backgrounds for cohesive branding.

    Primary accent color, providing a calming and natural aesthetic in headers, footers, and interactive elements.

  • White (#ffffff): Background for main content sections to maximize legibility.

    Background for main and images, focusing attention on the content.

  • Dark Charcoal (#333333): Main text color, providing contrast on lighter backgrounds.

    Used for body text and headings to ensure readability.

  • Medium Green (#3CB371): Hover color for interactive elements like links and buttons.

    Enhances interactivity, creating a subtle hover effect for navigation links and buttons.

  • Light Gray Border (#ccc): Borders for input fields and images to provide subtle definition.

    Outlines input fields and images to enhance form structure and visual separation.

Font:

Primary Font: Roboto, imported from Google Fonts, provides a modern, clean typography style that aligns with the minimalist theme.

  • Roboto 400 (Regular, 400 weight): Used for main body text, paragraphs, and standard content.

    This weight is applied to body text, paragraphs, and list items for readability and clean design.

  • Roboto 700 (Bold, 700 weight): Used for headers, subheaders, and emphasis in key sections.

    Provides emphasis in headings, navigation links, and bolded labels, such as in form fields or sections needing attention.

Typography and Layout

  • Header: Dark green background (#2E8B57) with white text and Roboto Bold (700) for the title.
  • Body Content: Dark charcoal text (#333333) with Roboto Regular (400) for body paragraphs on a white background (#ffffff).
  • Navigation Links: White text with Roboto Bold (700) on a dark green background (#2E8B57), with a hover effect in medium green (#3CB371).
  • Footer: Consistent dark green background (#2E8B57) with white text, using Roboto Regular (400) for information text.
